I Love Motorcycle

Post by Reed Motoblog, Nov 15, 2022.
Z1000R

If you are looking for a powerful bike, the Kawasaki Z1000-R-Edition is a powerful bike in the series. It comes with an upgraded engine which produces more power than other two-wheelers. The style and the wild design element go well with its overall looks. The suspension is excellent, and the maintenance cost is also budget-friendly. It has been over six months and counting, and I enjoyed every ride. The Kawasaki Z1000-R-Edition looks impressive at its front end due to the LED DRLs and projector headlamps. The thing that impressed me is the brand has designed exhaust pipes on both sides along the rear fender.

My new KAWASAKI ZX6r Motorcycle

Post by Jao Moto, Oct 31, 2022.
ZX6r

If you are fond of superbikes, then the new variant of Kawasaki Ninja-ZX-6R is the best variant that you can get. I still remember my first long ride the moment you start the engine; it starts flying. The Kawasaki Ninja-ZX-6R is best for long rides. The outer body look is one of the best things Kawasaki has upgraded. The suspension is excellent, and the maintenance cost is also budget-friendly. It has been over five months and counting, and I haven't faced any issues till now. Overall, starting from the engine, look, and to the pick-up, I enjoyed every single ride of it.
